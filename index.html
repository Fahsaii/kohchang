KOH CHANG

Koh Chang used to be an island without settlement communities. But it is important as a port for boats to escape the monsoon winds. A source of food and fresh water, especially at Ao Salak Phet or Ao Salad, is well known among pirates, Hainan Chinese and Vietnamese people. Currently on Koh Chang there are 8 villages inhabited.

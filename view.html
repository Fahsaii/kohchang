VIEW POINT

For those who go to Koh Chang, Trat Province and want to go up and see the beautiful view. of the sea from above Recommended to check in. Kai Bae View Point which is like a landmark of Koh Chang The viewpoint has 2 floors, we have to walk up the hill to the top of the viewpoint. Along the way, we will see the view of Kai Bae Beach as well as the 4 large and small islands, namely Koh Man Nai, Koh Man Nok, Koh Yuak and Koh Pli, which they say that the higher you walk, the higher you go. The view will be even more beautiful. Get to see the full 180-degree view ever.

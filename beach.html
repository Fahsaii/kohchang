WHITE SAND BEACH

White Sand Beach, located in Koh Chang District, Trat Province, is a famous and well-known beach on Koh Chang. Also, the ferry terminal is only 8 kilometers away.The beach front is 2.5 kilometers long, and the sea water is beautiful and clear without dangerous rocks. cause tourists to visit White Sand Beach on a regular basis, sure enough. The surrounding area will have many restaurants and accommodations. waiting to serve tourists in full as well. Another highlight of this beach is that we can see the sunset.
